DerewolPrint

SaaS Product

Print Management, Reimagined

A full SaaS combining a desktop Electron app and a PWA. Print shops generate a QR code — customers scan, upload files, and send to the printer. Tokens expire in 30 minutes for security.

Try the PWA GitHub
How it works
1
The print shop opens the Electron desktop app and generates a QR code for their printer.
2
The customer scans the QR code on their phone, opening a secure 30-minute session.
3
The customer uploads files. They are sent directly to the printer and deleted after printing.
